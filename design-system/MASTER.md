## Design Principles

### Critical

- **Booking CTA buttons must use high-contrast warm colors (orange/amber) with minimum 44px touch target**
  - _Warm colors create urgency and emotional engagement; large touch targets ensure mobile usability for booking actions_

- **Price display must support multi-currency formatting with clear per-night or per-stay labeling**
  - _International travelers need prices in their preferred currency; ambiguous pricing causes booking abandonment_

- **Show availability status with clear color coding: green=available, yellow=limited, red=sold out**
  - _Instant visual recognition of availability reduces search time and improves the booking decision process_

- **Use serif fonts for hero headings and hotel names to convey hospitality warmth**
  - _Serif fonts convey tradition and warmth; appropriate for hospitality branding and creating emotional connection_

- **Booking flow must show running total at every step with clear cost breakdown**
  - _Price transparency throughout the booking process builds trust and reduces cart abandonment_

- **All interactive elements must have minimum 44x44px touch targets for mobile guests**
  - _Travel applications are primarily used on mobile devices; small touch targets cause frustration and errors_

- **Guest check-in forms must be mobile-optimized and completable within 90 seconds**
  - _Long check-in processes frustrate guests; mobile optimization is essential as most travelers use smartphones_

### Recommended

- **Use warm color palette (orange/amber/gold) as primary to create hospitality atmosphere**
  - _Warm colors evoke comfort and invitation; appropriate for hotel and travel brand experiences_

- **Room cards must display hero image, price, rating, and key amenities at minimum**
  - _Travelers make quick decisions based on visual appeal and essential information_

- **Date pickers must show pricing per night inline and highlight best-value dates**
  - _Flexible travelers appreciate seeing prices across dates to find the best deal_

- **Display guest reviews with star ratings and verified-stay badges**
  - _Social proof through reviews is the most influential factor in hotel booking decisions_

- **Use large, high-quality images throughout the booking experience**
  - _Visual content is the primary decision driver for travel_

- **Support autofill for returning guest information in booking forms**
  - _Returning guests should have a faster booking experience_

- **Provide clear cancellation policy information before payment step**
  - _Transparent cancellation policies build trust and reduce post-booking support requests_
